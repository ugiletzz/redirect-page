How to Spot Real Profiles on Dating Sites

Worried about fake accounts? You’re not alone. Here are some quick tips to identify real people on dating platforms:

  • Photos: Real users usually have multiple photos, not stock images.
  • Language: Be cautious of overly generic or robotic messages.
  • Social Links: Real profiles often link to social media or have mutual interests listed.
  • Ask to Video Chat: It’s the fastest way to confirm someone is real.

Always trust your instincts, and don’t be afraid to walk away if something feels off.
